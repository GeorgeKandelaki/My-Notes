## My Own Workout Plan
- **REST** - **90-180s**
- **REP RANGE** - **6 - 10**
- Go Till **FAILURE** on every exercise
- Always think about the **FORM**
- Execute the exercise with **PERFECT FORM**
- We are training for **STRENGTH**, **NOT** for _looks_.
- Rule of Controlling the Weight
  **1-2-1 Rule**:
   - 2 seconds on the way down
   - Pause for a second
   - Then Go All the way up
   - Pause for a second
   - 2 seconds on the way down
